## About
In oder to excite efficiently a transducer we must have a high voltage (around -100V high) short time (half the period of the central frequency of the transducer) excitating pulse.

* [`ITF-H_neg_12v`]()
* [`ITF-I_pulse_on`]()
* -100V

### Output

* -100V pulse
